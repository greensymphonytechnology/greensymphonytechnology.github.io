Get to know about our company

Green Symphony Technology Sdn Bhd or “GSTSB” was formerly known as Bumi Earth Corporation Sdn Bhd and was established on 09 July 2009. GSTSB is a start-up organization in Melaka, Malaysia that offers energy saving solution and green technology solution for public and private sectors.

Registered as an Energy Service Company (ESCo) with Malaysia Energy Commission (ST), our team of specialist provides consulting and engineering services to industrial, commercials and government sectors. We are specializing in a wide range of green technology, commercial/residential indoor and outdoor lighting.
