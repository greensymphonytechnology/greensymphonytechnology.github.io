Consulting & Engineering

Green Symphony Technology Sdn Bhd is a Consulting and Engineering company experience in the development and provision of services for Energy Sustainability.

Registered as an Energy Service Company (ESCo) with Malaysia Energy Commission (ST), our team of specialist provides consulting and engineering services to industrial, commercials and government sectors. The experience of our consultants will guide you along the path to sustainability, from strategic decisions to the realization energy efficiency plans.

The consulting services provided by us are designed to support in the sustainable development strategies that our clients have decided to implement. Generally, we are able to assist in the analysis and planning of energy strategies and sustainability as well licensing and compliance to the legislation via the services provided.

Project Implementation

We will assist in the design and implementation of conservation projects, taking care of the technical aspects and authorization, helping in identifying the best technologies and coordinating installers of confidence.

Plus, we also perform planning, analyzing, organizing activities as well as works direction and project management. Therefore, we will cooperate pro-actively to the growth plan, providing specialized services, decision-making systems and technological tools designed to facilitate efficiency. This allows our clients to reduce operating costs and environmental impact while increasing profitability.

Carbon Management

Carbon Management means the management of CO2 emissions and other Greenhouse Gas. Managing emissions is a good practice that allows the reductions of operating costs, improve the supply chain, reduce financial and regulatory risks and explore new opportunities and markets.

The reduction in the availability of natural resources requires companies and government agencies to review their procurement strategies and energy sustainability. Sustainability is synonymous with economic performance as well as environmental protection.

We aspire to carry a reputation in the marketplace for developing and delivering high quality energy efficient solutions for use in residential, commercial, industrial and governmental applications. We can achieve this by maintaining a close understanding of market trends and needs.

Energy Efficiency Management

The best way and most cost-effective to increase the sustainability of its activities is to study possible ways of reducing energy consumption. In fact, it is much cheaper to invest in energy efficiency measures before investing in power plants from alternative or renewable sources. The usual approaches are as follows:

a.  Performing Energy Diagnosis / Audits
b.  Establishing Energy Management Systems
c.  Monitoring and Control Systems
d.  Energy saving measures



The big advantage, true soul ESCo, is the synergy with the advisory activities, which allows a single partner to optimize the project from an economic point of view, guide through the authorization procedures and the preparation of the practices in order to obtain the incentives. We are also able to support in obtaining the necessary funding for the implementation of the intervention by means of specific EPCs (Energy Performance Contracting).
